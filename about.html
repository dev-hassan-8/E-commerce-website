Since 2010

Design-led furniture crafted for every room.

Furniro began as a small studio with a simple belief: beautiful furniture should be accessible, sustainable, and crafted to last. Today, our teams across artisans, designers, and logistics partners continue to obsess over thoughtful materials and refined finishes.

We partner with responsible wood suppliers, work directly with experienced upholsterers, and iterate designs with customer feedback to ensure every piece feels timeless yet ready for modern living.
